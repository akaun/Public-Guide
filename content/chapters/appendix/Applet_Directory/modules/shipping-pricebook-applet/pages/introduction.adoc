== 1.1 When will Shipping Pricebook Applet be Used?

Sometimes, when a person makes an order on any e-commerce site,  the items ordered are from multiple suppliers so when you come to the shopping cart, all orders are sinked into 1 order with all the items from different vendors , some of them may have a shipping fee, some may not. In this scenario, a shipping pricebook applet can be used to sync your order/shopping cart or split your order/shopping cart, you can manage to charge for the items ordered separately (item by item) or all items can be aggregated into one fee.

== 1.2 Why should You Use A Shipping Pricebook Applet?

According to an article by BigCommerce, 54% of customers will cancel an order if the shipping costs are too high because  customers these days have high expectations regarding shipping. This shows that using a shipping software doesn’t just benefit the businesses, it also helps your customers.

When you use a shipping software, you’ll have access to up-to-date shipping information from the most popular carriers. This eliminates the guesswork on your end and ensures your pricing information is accurate. Your software will present you with shipping options from a variety of carriers. This allows you to compare your options and choose the one that’s the best fit for your customer.Businesses may be hesitant to invest in shipping software because of the added cost to their  business but if you ship more than a few packages a week, you may actually end up saving money on postage. Using a shipping price software will also save your valuable time. Since you can print labels and packing slips from your computer, you can skip the weekly trip to the post office. It will also ensure that you have adequate inventory to fulfill your shipping orders. 

== 1.3 What are The Benefits of Shipping Pricebook Applet?

a. It makes it easy for the business to control and monitor the users

b. Users can monitor and control shipping details (no of items, address, payment, volume of the order, )

c. You can have integration between order-taking software, customer relationship management (CRM) software, inventory management software, or anything else that requires access to shipping data. 

d. It has a customer support system to cater unhappy customers

e. It gives you access to APIs that allow you to remain in sync with incoming orders while processing current ones.

f. It gives you comprehensive reporting and analytics system with real-time information like the time taken for parcels to deliver, the number of undelivered orders, and sync orders to help you analyze your shipping operations
