= 7.0 My Device

.Register a device
image::register_device.jpg[Register a device, 300, 658, align="center"]

The user can register their mobile device as a “Stock Take Device” over here for the first time. If you have not registered a device before, click the “Register Device” button to register your device. 

.View device details
image::device_details.jpg[View device details, 300, 658, align="center"]

After registering a device, you will be able to view your device details. Through the interface, the user can change their device's name alone. After making changes to the device name, click “Save Changes.”.
